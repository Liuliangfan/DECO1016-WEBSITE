Derby Day fashion 2019: All the hot looks and frock shocks
Celebrities have descended on Melbourne’s Flemington Racecourse, all dressed up in their racing finest for this year’s Derby Day. There’s plenty of money at stake as the action plays out on the course – but for most of us, it’s all about what’s going on trackside. Derby Day is all about the monochrome, so as usual, it’s a sea of white and black among A-list guests. But standing out from the crowd was Phoebe Burgess, who made an unexpected appearance trackside at Lexus’ marquee.
